11  Learning & Upskilling Module

11.1 Purpose

The Learning Module ensures that every team member continuously grows their skills, knowledge, and confidence. Our team comes from diverse backgrounds â€” some members are learning technical concepts and English language simultaneously. This module is designed to meet everyone where they are and take them further.

â€œTell me and I forget. Show me and I remember. Involve me and I understand.â€

Our learning approach:

Key Principles:

  1. Learn by doing â€” Every concept is tied to a real task
  2. Small daily doses â€” 15-30 minutes of learning every day
  3. AI-assisted but human-verified â€” Use AI to learn, verify with practice
  4. No judgment â€” Every question is valid, every pace is respected
  5. Multi-language support â€” Learn in whatever language works best, document in English (with AI help)

11.3 Learning Structure

11.3.1 Daily Learning (15 minutes)

Part of the daily standup â€” the last 5-10 minutes are dedicated to learning:

Daily Learning Schedule
Day Focus Area Format
Monday Hardware Concept 5-min explainer by Team Lead or video
Tuesday SOP Deep-Dive Walk through one SOP section in detail
Wednesday Tool/AI Training Practice using a tool or AI assistant

11.3.2 Weekly Deep-Dive (1 hour)

Every week, one focused session:

  • Hands-on workshop on a specific skill
  • Guest speaker (internal or external)
  • Video tutorial followed by discussion
  • Practice exercise with Maker-Checker

11.3.3 Monthly Assessment

  • Informal skills check (not an exam â€” a guided conversation)
  • Review of learning log entries
  • Goal setting for next month
  • Celebration of progress

11.8 Mentorship & Buddy System

11.8.1 Buddy Pairs

  • Pair experienced members with newer members
  • Buddies work together on tasks
  • Natural Maker-Checker pairing
  • Rotate buddies monthly for broader learning

11.8.2 Team Lead as Coach

The Team Lead:

  • Identifies learning gaps through daily observation
  • Creates targeted mini-lessons
  • Provides encouragement and constructive feedback
  • Connects team with resources (videos, AI tools, external help)
  • Tracks progress without creating pressure
