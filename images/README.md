# Images for Hardware Visual Guide

This folder contains reference photos for the Hardware & Equipment Visual Guide chapter.

## Required Images

Download the following images and save them with these exact filenames:

### Servers
1. **server-front.jpg** - Server front view showing drive bays, power button, LEDs
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Wikimedia_Foundation_Servers-8055_35.jpg
   - What to show: Drive bays (numbered slots), power button, status LEDs, front bezel

2. **server-rear.jpg** - Server rear view showing PSUs, ports, fans
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Wikimedia_Foundation_Servers-8055_02.jpg
   - What to show: 2 PSU slots, NIC ports, management port, fan vents

### Cables
3. **cable-rj45.jpg** - RJ45 ethernet connector close-up
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Ethernet_RJ45_connector_p1160054.jpg
   - What to show: Clear plastic clip, 8 colored wires visible

4. **cable-c13.jpg** - C13/C14 power connector
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:IEC-60320-C13.jpg
   - What to show: 3-prong rectangular connector

5. **cable-c19.jpg** - C19/C20 high-power connector
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:IEC_60320_C19.jpg
   - What to show: Wider than C13, high-power connector

6. **cable-dac.jpg** - DAC (Direct Attach Copper) cable with modules
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:SFP%2B_Direct-Attach_Copper_cable.jpg
   - What to show: Thick black cable, metal modules at both ends

7. **cable-fiber.jpg** - Fiber optic cable (AOC)
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Fibre_optic_illuminated.jpg
   - What to show: Thin, flexible, often orange/yellow

### Modules
8. **module-sfp.png** - SFP transceiver module
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:SFP_optic.png
   - What to show: Small metal module, thumb-size

9. **module-qsfp.jpg** - QSFP transceiver module
   - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:QSFP-Plus-Transceiver.jpg
   - What to show: Wider than SFP, two-thumb size

### Rack
10. **rack-datacenter.jpg** - Server racks in datacenter
    - Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Racks_of_servers.jpg
    - What to show: Vertical rack structure, equipment at different heights

## How to Download from Wikimedia Commons

1. Click the source link above
2. On the Wikimedia page, look for "Original file" or "Full resolution" link
3. Right-click the image and "Save Image As..."
4. Save with the exact filename shown above
5. Place in this `/images/` folder

## Alternative Sources

If Wikimedia images don't work, search for Creative Commons or public domain images:
- https://unsplash.com/ (free to use)
- https://pixabay.com/ (free to use)
- Google Images with "Labeled for reuse" filter

Just ensure the image clearly shows what's described in the "What to show" section.
